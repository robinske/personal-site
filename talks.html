Hello!

I started public speaking at small events around 2013, and started doing larger conference talks in 2016. Some of the things I like to talk about include Scala, Functional Programming, building teams, how programmers learn and develop, and how programming communities thrive.

I'm currently based in San Francisco, and excited to travel for speaking engagements. My schedule is limited however, so more notice is better if I would have to travel far.

When I'm submitting to a CFP or thinking about attending a conference, these are the things I look for:

  • Does your conference have a Code of Conduct and a plan to enforce it?
  • Are past or invited speakers people I admire?
  • Does the conference cover speaker travel & hotel?
  • Does the conference support diversity programs, or initiatives to make new attendees feel welcome?
  • Will the talks be recorded (in a way that I could share with people that can't attend?)

Please get in touch if you are looking for speakers at your event! I'm happy to prepare a new talk or give one that has been tested before. Below you can find some of my recent talks.
